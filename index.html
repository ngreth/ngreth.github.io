Austin Marathon Exploratory Data Analysis

Garmin, a GPS watch brand, provides all collected data to users. With my goal of running the Austin Marathon in less than 3 hours, I leveraged this data by performing an exploratory data analysis to gain more insight into my training and progress.

Kelly Blue Book Web Scraping Analysis

To see the changes in the used and new car market, I scraped data from KBB over time. By monitoring the trend of used and new vehicle prices, I determine whether it is worth buying a used or new vehicle at a given time.

Austin Metrobike Analysis

The City of Austin provides a wide variety of data to the public from Austin Metrobike, including detailed data about each time a bike is ridden. In order to understand where people ride the bikes and how often they ride the bikes, I performed an exploratory data analysis.
